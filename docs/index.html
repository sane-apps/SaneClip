Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

A clipboard manager that respects your privacy and stays out of your way.

Touch ID Protection

Lock your clipboard history with biometric authentication. Keep sensitive data like passwords and keys protected.

Lightning Shortcuts

⌘Shift+V opens history. ⌘1-9 pastes instantly. No mouse needed—your hands stay on the keyboard.

Pin Important Items

Keep SSH keys, code snippets, and frequently-used text always accessible. Pinned items never expire.

Unlimited History

Stores everything you copy, with smart word and character counts. Find that code snippet from last week instantly.

Native macOS Design

Glass effects, smooth animations, and dark mode support. Looks like it belongs on your Mac because it does.

Private by Design

100% on-device. No cloud sync. No analytics. No network calls. Your clipboard stays on your Mac.
